Button 按钮 ​

常用的操作按钮。

基础用法 ​

使用 type、plain、 round和 circle来定义按钮的样式。

<template>
  <p>
    <ym-button>Default</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary">Primary</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="success">Success</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="info">Info</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="warning">Warning</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="danger">Danger</ym-button>
  </p>

  <p>
    <ym-button plain>Plain</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary" plain>Primary</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="success" plain>Success</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="info" plain>Info</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="warning" plain>Warning</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="danger" plain>Danger</ym-button>
  </p>

  <p>
    <ym-button round>Round</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary" round>Primary</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="success" round>Success</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="info" round>Info</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="warning" round>Warning</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="danger" round>Danger</ym-button>
  </p>

  <p>
    <ym-button icon="search" circle />
    <ym-button type="primary" icon="edit" circle />
    <ym-button type="success" icon="check" circle />
    <ym-button type="info" icon="message" circle />
    <ym-button type="warning" icon="star" circle />
    <ym-button type="danger" icon="trash" circle />
  </p>
  
  <p>
    <ym-button corrugation>Corrugation</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary" corrugation>Primary</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="success" corrugation>Success</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="info" corrugation>Info</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="warning" corrugation>Warning</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="danger" corrugation>Danger</ym-button>
  </p>
</template>

禁用状态 ​

你可以使用 disabled 属性来定义按钮是否被禁用。

<template>
  <p>
    <ym-button>Default</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary">Primary</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="success">Success</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="info">Info</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="warning">Warning</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="danger">Danger</ym-button>
  </p>
  <p>
    <ym-button disabled>Default</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary" disabled>Primary</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="success" disabled>Success</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="info" disabled>Info</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="warning" disabled>Warning</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="danger" disabled>Danger</ym-button>
  </p>
</template>

图标按钮 ​

使用 icon属性来定义按钮的图标。

<template>
  <div>
    <ym-button type="primary" icon="edit" />
    <ym-button type="primary" icon="share" />
    <ym-button type="primary" icon="trash" />
    <ym-button type="primary" icon="search">Search</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary">
      Upload<ym-icon icon="upload" style="margin-left: 8px" />
    </ym-button>
  </div>
</template>

按钮组 ​

使用 <ym-button-group> 对多个按钮分组。

<template>
  <p>
    <ym-button-group type="success">
      <ym-button icon="arrow-left">上一页</ym-button>
      <ym-button >
        下一页<ym-icon icon="arrow-right" style="margin-left: 8px" />
      </ym-button>
    </ym-button-group>
  </p>
  <p>
    <ym-button-group disabled>
      <ym-button type="primary" icon="arrow-left">Previous Page</ym-button>
      <ym-button type="primary">
        Next Page<ym-icon icon="arrow-right" style="margin-left: 8px" />
      </ym-button>
    </ym-button-group>
  </p>
  <p>
    <ym-button-group type="warning" size="small">
      <ym-button type="primary" icon="edit" round />
      <ym-button type="primary" icon="share" />
      <ym-button type="primary" icon="trash" round />
    </ym-button-group>
  </p>
</template>

加载状态 ​

使用 loading属性来定义按钮的加载状态。

TIP

您可以使用 loading 插槽或 loadingIcon 属性自定义您的 loading 图标

ps: loading 插槽优先级高于 loadingIcon 属性

<template>
  <div>
    <ym-button type="primary" loading>Loading</ym-button>
    <ym-button type="primary" loading-icon="circle-notch" loading
      >Loading</ym-button
    >
    <ym-button type="primary" loading>
      <template #loading>
        <ym-icon style="margin-right: 6px" icon="star" spin />
      </template>
      Loading
    </ym-button>
  </div>
</template>

<template>

    <ym-button size="small">Small</ym-button>
    <ym-button size="large" icon="search">Search</ym-button>
    <ym-button icon="search">Search</ym-button>
    <ym-button size="small" icon="search">Search</ym-button>
  </p>
  <p>
    <ym-button size="large" round>Large</ym-button>
    <ym-button round>Default</ym-button>
    <ym-button size="small" round>Small</ym-button>
    <ym-button size="large" icon="search" round>Search</ym-button>
    <ym-button icon="search" round>Search</ym-button>
    <ym-button size="small" icon="search" round>Search</ym-button>
  </p>
  <p>
    <ym-button size="large" icon="search" circle />
    <ym-button icon="search" circle />
    <ym-button size="small" icon="search" circle />
  </p>
</template>

Tag ​

可以自定义元素标签。例如，按钮，div，路由链接，nuxt 链接。

div
a 

<template>
  <ym-button>button</ym-button>
  <ym-button tag="div" role="button" tabindex="0">div</ym-button>
  <ym-button
    type="primary"
    tag="a"
    href="#"
    target="_blank"
    rel="noopener noreferrer"
  >
    a
  </ym-button>
</template>

节流模式 ​

可以通过 useThrottle 属性来定义按钮是否使用节流模式 默认为 true。

<script setup>
function handleBtnClick() {
  // ErMessage.info("btn click");
}
</script>

<template>
  <p>
    <ym-button @click="handleBtnClick"> with throttle</ym-button>
    <ym-button :use-throttle="false" @click="handleBtnClick"
      >without throttle</ym-button
    >
  </p>
</template>
